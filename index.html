About

Hi there, I’m glad you found me. My name is John Rubenson and I like to make music.

On this page you can find out what I have been up to lately and maybe even a little bit about where I will go in the future. For me, the greatest thing about being a musician is being in the position to inspire other people. I take such pleasure in hearing that people have been motivated to create after hearing my music, whether it be a painting, a poem, their own music or something completely different. Music is not a one way street, it is a conversation where the listener's role is as important as the artist's. Well, here you can find my side of it all. What is yours?
